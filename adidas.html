ADIDAS PROFILE

01
Adidas Head Office,
Dubai Design District

For the Adidas MENA region headquarters design,I undertook a comprehensive revamping project that spanned across two floors. My approach seamlessly blended traditional and modern elements, creating a unique and engaging workspace. I focused on enhancing the mood and design through close collaboration with the client, integrating regional artworks and color themes to reflect the local context.

Key improvements included optimizing overall lighting, utilizing previously overlooked pockets of space, and refining spatial planning to minimize the waste of usable area. These changes aimed to create a more functional and aesthetically pleasing environment for the Adidas team.

02
Adidas PopUp, Terminal 1
Dubai Airport

For the Adidas pop-up store at Dubai Airport Terminal A,
I designed a compact yet striking retail space that captures the attention of the bustling airport crowd. The store features an innovative perforated screen façade, which adds visual interest and intrigue as travelers pass by. This design element not only enhances the store's presence but also creates a dynamic and engaging experience for visitors, effectively drawing them into the compact space amidst the busy terminal environment.

03
Adidas HOS,
Cape Town

At one of Cape Town's most prominent locations, I had the opportunity to develop one of the first Home of Sport (HOS) concepts in the country, situated within Canal Walk Mall. This project involved a multifaceted approach to design, making the store both intriguing and accessible from various entry points. I dedicated significant effort to understanding and implementing the HOS concept throughout the façade, ensuring it aligned seamlessly with the spatial plan of the space. By integrating innovative design elements and optimizing the store’s layout, I aimed to create a unique and immersive experience for visitors. The result is a space that not only showcases the Home of Sport brand but also enhances the overall shopping experience within the mall.

04
Adidas Halo,
Dezingof, Israel

For the Adidas Halo flagship store in Israel, I designed the first flagship location in the country, featuring an expansive floor plate to accommodate diverse store segments. A key aspect of the design was the creation of distinct, engaging zones, including dedicated areas for women’s apparel, football gear, Originals, and more. I placed significant emphasis on the spatial aesthetics and functionality of these zones to enhance the store’s overall atmosphere. The façade's dynamic curvature plays a pivotal role, as it presents a striking and continuous view of the store from the road, effectively drawing attention and showcasing the brand’s identity.

05
Adidas Pulse FO,
Dubai Outlet Village

The Pulse Factory Outlet stands as the largest store of its kind in the region, strategically located in the prominent Dubai Outlet Village. One of the key challenges I faced was adapting the design to harmonize with the existing elevation style, which required a creative approach to the façade. By reimagining and integrating the store’s design within the established architectural context, I was able to enhance its visual appeal while maintaining coherence with the surrounding environment. The result is a striking and cohesive façade that elevates the store’s presence within the outlet village.

06
Adidas KIDS
Flagship Store

The Kids Store Concept represents the first flagship store for children in the region, designed with a fresh material palette to reflect the vibrant and playful nature of its young audience. I focused on creating an engaging environment that enhances the store’s lively atmosphere. A notable feature of the design is the cash desk backwall, which we developed in collaboration with renowned Italian architect Arturo Tedeschi. This dynamic, parametric design element adds a unique architectural dimension to the store, highlighting its status as a flagship location and enriching the overall customer experience.

07
Adidas
Maker Lab,
Dubai Mall

For the Customization Zone within the Maker Lab, I undertook the design of bespoke furniture tailored to enhance the customer experience. This area allows patrons to bring in their personal items for customization by artists, creating a unique and interactive environment. The furniture design was crafted to facilitate seamless interactions between customers and artists while reflecting the zone's creative and personalized nature. The result is a thoughtfully designed space that supports the customization process and contributes to an engaging and memorable experience for visitors.

07
Adidas
Marina Bay Sands,
Singapore

The Home of Sport flagship store at Marina Bay Sands, a premier tourist destination, features a striking design with a prominent curving façade. The design includes a central column that we transformed into a captivating art element, serving both an aesthetic and functional purpose. This innovative approach not only draws customers' attention but also optimizes the space by incorporating a footwear trial area. The dynamic façade and artistic column create a visually compelling presence, enhancing the store's appeal and elevating the overall shopping experience at this vibrant location.

07
Adidas Originals,
Mall of the Emirates

The Originals Concept store stands as the first of its kind in the UAE and the Middle East, meticulously designed to align with the brand's concept and guidelines. To infuse a regional context, I incorporated a unique design element inspired by the sand dunes, creating a distinctive visual feature. The contour art, inspired by sand dune formations, is prominently displayed on the back of the footwear walls and enhanced with cove lighting to accentuate its intricate levels. I personally drafted the dune lines, from initial sketches to the final model, and am proud to see this design element vividly brought to life in the store.

07
Adidas
Originals Flagship,
Dubai Mall

The Originals Concept store, located in Dubai Mall, is one of the largest flagship stores for Originals globally and the first of its kind in the world. Dedicated exclusively to Originals products and merchandise, it also features high-profile collaborations such as Y-3. As part of the design development team, I focused on revamping the customer activation zones, creating diverse concepts and moods to enhance the spatial experience. This involved designing engaging areas that reflect the brand's identity and facilitate an immersive shopping environment, aligning with the store's prestigious status and innovative offerings.
