ELECTRIC CAR PLANT

Brief: The design project focuses on creating an automobile manufacturing plant that accommodates both fuel and electric vehicle production. It emphasizes implementing comprehensive safety measures and service provisions to ensure secure and efficient operations for both manufacturing output and staff.

Aim: The proposed design aims to advance the automotive industry by integrating alternative energy sources, fostering economic growth, and promoting environmental awareness. By providing a platform for innovation, the plant will facilitate the shift towards electric vehicles, highlighting the importance of renewable energy and addressing environmental degradation.

Abstract: The project focuses on spatial reorganization to enhance production conditions, informed by research from companies like IBM, Renault, and Olivetti. These companies have explored future factory concepts with expert input.
